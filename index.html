GESS RNG Biogas USA, LLC, a Delaware corporation, (the “Company”) is a developer of agricultural waste to energy plants producing renewable natural gas (RNG) from farms in Idaho, Missouri, and North Carolina. The Company specializes in the development of anaerobic digester plants using swine and dairy manure, as well as row crop residues.
