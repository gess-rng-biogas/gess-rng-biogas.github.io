Why GESS RNG Biogas

We strive to achieve global renewable energy goals while excelling in providing state-of-the-art waste management solutions. GESS RNG Biogas works towards establishing a better future especially with the ravaging of our global climatic conditions. We utilize agricultural wastes from dairy and swine farms, and food wastes converting them to carbon-neutral renewable natural gas (RNG). This RNG produced - is an extremely versatile bio-energy source and fully compatible considering the existing U.S. pipeline infrastructure.

With the constant need to meet federal and state regulatory levels for polluting elements such as Carbon-dioxide (CO2) and Methane (CH4) contributing to the overall Greenhouse effect, GESS RNG Biogas plays a vital role in helping industries meet their carbon footprint goals. The future of technology and specifically energy, relies more towards a carbon negative energy source. This puts GESS RNG Biogas in a firm position to provide the necessary technology and consulting solutions with a team of highly skilled and seasoned individuals having worked on numerous projects. We employ the best waste management practices utilizing proven Digester and Purification technologies along with efficient Upgrading equipment.

GESS RNG Biogas provides the opportunity for investments with attractive financial returns for businesses transitioning their energy needs in the marketplace. We also strive towards driving change by changing public and government expectations in efforts to encourage a “Greener” attitude.
